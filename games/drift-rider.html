How to Play

Controls

  • Arrow Keys or WASD: Control your vehicle
  • W/Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • S/Down Arrow: Brake/Reverse
  • A/Left Arrow: Steer left
  • D/Right Arrow: Steer right
  • Space: Handbrake (for drifting)
  • R: Reset car position
  • Esc: Pause menu

Gameplay Tips

  • Master the handbrake to initiate controlled drifts around corners
  • Release the accelerator briefly before corners for better control
  • Try to drift through turns rather than braking completely
  • Use the full width of the track to optimize your racing line
  • Complete races to earn coins for upgrades and new vehicles
  • Different cars have different handling characteristics - find the one that suits your style

About Drift Rider

Drift Rider is an adrenaline-pumping racing game that puts you behind the wheel of powerful drift cars. Unlike traditional racing games that focus solely on speed, Drift Rider emphasizes the art and skill of controlled drifting - the technique of intentionally oversteering while maintaining control through corners.

The game features a progression system where players can earn coins by completing races and challenges. These coins can be used to unlock new vehicles with different handling characteristics or upgrade existing cars with better engines, tires, and suspension systems. Each upgrade affects how your car handles, allowing you to customize your driving experience to match your style.

With multiple race tracks set in various environments from urban streets to mountain passes, Drift Rider offers diverse challenges that will test your driving skills. Each track has its own unique layout with sharp hairpins, long sweeping corners, and straight sections where you can build up speed before the next drift challenge.

Whether you're a casual player looking for quick racing thrills or a dedicated enthusiast aiming to master every track with perfect drift lines, Drift Rider delivers an engaging and satisfying driving experience that will keep you coming back for more high-octane action.
