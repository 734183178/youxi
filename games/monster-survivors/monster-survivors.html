About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense roguelike survival game inspired by classic arcade action and modern roguelite mechanics. Your objective is simple: survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters.

As you play, you'll face waves of increasingly difficult monsters while collecting experience points to level up and choose new weapons and abilities. Each run is unique, with procedurally generated enemy waves and randomized power-ups making every playthrough a fresh challenge.

Whether you have just a few minutes to spare or want to dive into an extended gaming session, Monster Survivors offers accessible yet deep gameplay that will keep you coming back for "just one more run."
