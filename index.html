Contract Content

RESIDENTIAL RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on this date between the following parties:

LANDLORD: _________________________ ("Landlord")

TENANT(S): _________________________ ("Tenant")

1. PROPERTY

Landlord hereby rents to Tenant and Tenant rents from Landlord for residential purposes only, the premises located at:

________________________________

________________________________

("Premises"), together with the following furnishings and appliances:

________________________________

2. TERM

This Agreement shall commence on _____________, 20___ and continue:

☐ as a month-to-month tenancy. Either party may terminate this Agreement by giving at least 30 days' written notice to the other party.

☐ for a fixed term ending on _____________, 20___.

3. RENT

Tenant shall pay $__________ per month, payable in advance on the _______ day of each month.

First month's rent: $__________

Rent shall be paid by:

☐ Cash ☐ Personal Check ☐ Money Order ☐ Credit Card ☐ Electronic Payment

Rent payments shall be made to:

________________________________

________________________________

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT

Upon execution of this Agreement, Tenant shall pay a security deposit in the amount of $__________ to be held by Landlord as security for Tenant's faithful performance of the terms of this Agreement.

5. UTILITIES

Tenant shall be responsible for the payment of the following utilities and services:

☐ Electricity ☐ Gas ☐ Water ☐ Sewage ☐ Trash Removal

☐ Cable TV ☐ Internet ☐ Phone ☐ Other: _________

Landlord shall be responsible for the payment of the following utilities and services:

☐ Electricity ☐ Gas ☐ Water ☐ Sewage ☐ Trash Removal

☐ Cable TV ☐ Internet ☐ Phone ☐ Other: _________

6. OCCUPANTS

The Premises shall be occupied by the following named persons only:

________________________________

________________________________

7. PETS

☐ No pets shall be kept on the Premises without the prior written consent of Landlord.

☐ Tenant may keep the following pets on the Premises:

________________________________

8. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS

Tenant shall maintain the Premises in a clean and sanitary manner and shall surrender the Premises at termination of the tenancy in as good condition as received, normal wear and tear excepted.

Tenant shall be responsible for repairs required due to Tenant's negligence or misuse.

Tenant shall promptly notify Landlord of any maintenance issues or necessary repairs.

9. ENTRY BY LANDLORD

Landlord shall have the right to enter the Premises:

a) In case of emergency

b) To make necessary repairs or improvements

c) To show the Premises to prospective tenants, purchasers, or lenders

d) When Tenant has abandoned or surrendered the Premises

Except in cases of emergency, Landlord shall give Tenant reasonable notice of intent to enter.

10. PROHIBITION AGAINST ASSIGNMENT

Tenant shall not assign this Agreement, or sublet any part of the Premises, without the prior written consent of Landlord.

11. TERMINATION

If Tenant fails to comply with any of the terms of this Agreement, Landlord may terminate this Agreement and proceed with eviction in accordance with applicable law.

12. SIGNATURES

By signing below, Tenant acknowledges having read and understood all of the terms and conditions of this Agreement and agrees to be bound by its terms.

Landlord:

_______________________

Date:

_______________________

Tenant:

_______________________

Date:

_______________________

Rental Arrangements
