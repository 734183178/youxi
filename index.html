About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where you must fend off waves of monsters in a post-apocalyptic world. Collect weapons, upgrade your abilities, and survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult enemies.

This fast-paced action game tests your reflexes and strategic thinking as you make split-second decisions to stay alive. With procedurally generated levels, no two games are ever the same!

Key Features

Endless Gameplay

Experience unique runs with procedurally generated levels that ensure endless replayability.

Powerful Upgrades

Collect various weapons and abilities to build your character into a monster-slaying machine.

Challenging Enemies

Face diverse enemy types with unique attack patterns and abilities.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Your weapons will automatically target nearby enemies. Collect experience points from defeated monsters to level up and choose new abilities. The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes.

Survive as long as possible, defeat powerful bosses, and compete for the highest score!

Tips for Beginners

• Focus on movement and avoiding enemies at the beginning

• Prioritize abilities that complement your playstyle

• Don't get cornered - always leave yourself an escape route

• Collect all experience orbs to level up faster
